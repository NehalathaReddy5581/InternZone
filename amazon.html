JOBS OFFERED BY

Amazon offers a variety of job opportunities for Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) students including internships and full-time roles. Here are some of the types of jobs available

  1. Software Development Engineer (SDE):
    Involved in designing, developing, and maintaining software solutions.
  2. Cloud Support Engineer:
    Provides technical support for Amazon Web Services (AWS) customers.
  3. Data Engineer:
    Focuses on building and managing data infrastructure and pipelines.
  4. Machine Learning :
    Develops and implements machine learning models and algorithms.
  5. Security
    Ensures the security of Amazon's products and services.
