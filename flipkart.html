JOBS OFFERED BY

Flipkart hires Computer Science graduates for various technical and non-technical roles, including:

  1. Software Development Roles (Engineering):
    • Software Development Engineer (SDE) I, II, III : Backend, Frontend, Full-Stack
    • Data Engineer : Big Data, ETL, Cloud Services
    • Machine Learning Engineer : AI/ML algorithms for recommendations and fraud detection
    • DevOps Engineer : CI/CD, Kubernetes, AWS, Infrastructure Automation

  2. Data Science & Analytics :
    • Data Scientist : Predictive modeling, deep learning, recommendation systems
    • Business Analyst : Data-driven insights for business decisions
    • Data Engineer : ETL pipelines, Big Data processing
  3. Product & Management Roles
    • Product Manager : Defines features and user experience for Flipkart’s platform
    • Program Manager : Handles project timelines and execution
  4. Security & IT Infrastructure
    • Security Engineer : Cybersecurity, threat analysis, penetration testing
    • Cloud Engineer : AWS, Azure, GCP-based solutions
  5. UX/UI & Frontend Development
    • UI/UX Designer : User experience and interface design
    • Frontend Engineer : Web and mobile app development (React.js, Angular)
  6. Supply Chain & Logistics Tech
    • Operations Research Engineer : Optimizes logistics and delivery
    • Warehouse Automation Engineer : Robotics and AI for smart fulfillment centers

Stock Options (ESOPs) at Flipkart:

Flipkart provides Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) to its employees, allowing them to acquire company shares at a predetermined price after a specified vesting period. The standard vesting schedule is over four years:

  • 1st Year: 25%
  • 2nd Year: 25%
  • 3rd Year: 25%
  • 4th Year: 25%

Unlike Restricted Stock Units (RSUs), ESOPs require employees to purchase the shares upon vesting, often at a price lower than the market value.

Sign-On Bonus:

A Sign-On Bonus is a one-time payment provided to new employees upon joining the company. At Flipkart, this bonus varies based on the role and experience level. For instance, an entry-level SDE I position may include a sign-on bonus of ₹300,000.
