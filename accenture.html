JOBS OFFERED BY

Accenture offers a variety of roles for B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (CSE) graduates, providing opportunities to work on cutting-edge technologies and innovative projects. Below is an overview of key positions, including role descriptions, required skills, and estimated salary ranges:

  1. Software Engineer
    • Role Overview: Develop, test, and maintain software applications tailored to client requirements. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality solutions.
    • Skills Required-
      ->Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++.
      ->Understanding of software development methodologies like Agile and Scrum.
      ->Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹4 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹36 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals.
  2. Application Development Analyst
    • Role Overview: Analyze client requirements and develop application solutions to meet business needs. Involve in designing, coding, testing, and deploying applications.
    • Skills Required-
      ->Experience with application development frameworks and tools.
      ->Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
      ->Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹5 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹15 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals.
  3. Application Developer
    • Role Overview: Design, build, and configure applications to meet business process and application requirements.
    • Skills Required-
      ->Proficiency in programming languages and development frameworks.
      ->Experience with database management systems.
      ->Strong debugging and problem-solving skills.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹4 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹16 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals.
  4. Consultant
    • Role Overview: Provide strategic advice to clients on technology solutions that will drive business success.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Strong understanding of business processes and technology integration.
      ->Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
      ->Ability to manage multiple stakeholders and projects.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹4 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹26 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals
  5. Team Leader
    • Role Overview: Lead a team of developers and analysts to deliver projects on time and within budget.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Strong leadership and team management skills.
      ->Experience in project planning and execution.
      ->Excellent problem-solving abilities.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹7 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹17 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals
  6. Associate Manager
    • Role Overview: Oversee project delivery, manage client relationships, and ensure alignment with business objectives.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Proven experience in project management and client engagement.
      ->Strong analytical and strategic thinking skills.
      ->Ability to manage budgets and resources effectively.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹13 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹27 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals
  7. Senior Analyst
    • Role Overview: Conduct in-depth analysis to support business decisions and project implementations.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Strong analytical and data interpretation skills.
      ->Proficiency in data analysis tools and software.
      ->Ability to communicate complex findings to non-technical stakeholders.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹6 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹13 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals.
  8. Program Analyst
    • Role Overview: Support program management activities, including planning, execution, and monitoring of projects.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Understanding of project management principles.
      ->Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
      ->Effective communication skills.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹3 lakhs per annum for freshers, with potential growth up to ₹12 lakhs per annum for experienced professionals.
