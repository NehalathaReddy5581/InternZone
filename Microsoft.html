JOBS OFFERED BY

Microsoft offers a diverse range of roles for B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (CSE) graduates, providing opportunities to work on innovative projects across various domains. Below is an overview of ten key positions, including role descriptions, required skills, and estimated salary ranges:

  1. Software Engineer
    • Role Overview: Design, develop, test, and maintain software applications and systems. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality products.
    • Skills Required
      ->Proficiency in programming languages such as C#, Java, or Python.
      ->Strong understanding of data structures and algorithms.
      ->Experience with software development methodologies.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹13.0 lakhs to ₹50.0 lakhs per annum, depending on experience and role.
  2. Technical Support Engineer
    • Role Overview: Provide technical assistance to customers by diagnosing and resolving software and hardware issues. Ensure customer satisfaction through effective problem-solving and communication.
    • Skills Required
      ->Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
      ->Excellent communication abilities.
      ->Familiarity with Microsoft products and services.
    • Salary Range: The typical base pay range for this role in the U.S. is USD $64,800 - $130,100 per year.
  3. Cloud Solutions Architect
    • Role Overview: Design and implement cloud-based solutions for clients, ensuring scalability, security, and performance. Collaborate with stakeholders to align cloud strategies with business objectives.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Proficiency in cloud platforms like Microsoft Azure.
      ->Experience with cloud architecture and services.
      ->Strong understanding of networking and security principles.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹20.0 lakhs to ₹40.0 lakhs per annum.
  4. Data Scientist
    • Role Overview: Analyze complex datasets to extract actionable insights. Develop predictive models and machine learning algorithms to solve business problems.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Proficiency in statistical analysis and machine learning techniques.
      ->Experience with data analysis tools like R or Python.
      ->Strong problem-solving skills.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹15.0 lakhs to ₹35.0 lakhs per annum.
  5. Product Manager
    • Role Overview: Oversee the development and launch of products. Define product vision, gather requirements, and collaborate with engineering, design, and marketing teams.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Strong understanding of market trends and user needs.
      ->Excellent project management and communication skills.
      ->Ability to work cross-functionally and lead teams.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹20.0 lakhs to ₹45.0 lakhs per annum.
  6. UX Designer
    • Role Overview: Design intuitive and engaging user interfaces. Conduct user research and create wireframes, prototypes, and visual designs.
    • Skills Required: Proficiency in design tools like Adobe XD, Figma, or Sketch. Strong understanding of user-centered design principles. Ability to translate user needs into functional designs.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹12.0 lakhs to ₹30.0 lakhs per annum.
  7. DevOps Engineer
    • Role Overview: Implement and manage continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines. Automate processes to enhance operational efficiency.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Knowledge of DevOps tools and practices.
      ->Experience with cloud platforms such as Azure.
      ->Strong scripting skills in languages like Bash or Python.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹15.0 lakhs to ₹32.0 lakhs per annum.
  8. Security Engineer
    • Role Overview: Identify and mitigate security vulnerabilities in systems and applications. Develop security protocols and conduct regular security assessments.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Knowledge of cybersecurity principles and practices.
      ->Experience with security tools and technologies.
      ->Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹16.0 lakhs to ₹34.0 lakhs per annum.
  9. Machine Learning Engineer
    • Role Overview: Develop and deploy machine learning models and algorithms. Work on projects involving natural language processing, computer vision, and predictive analytics.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Proficiency in machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
      ->Strong programming skills in Python or Java.
      ->Experience with data preprocessing and model optimization.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹18.0 lakhs to ₹38.0 lakhs per annum.
  10. Network Engineer
    • Role Overview: Design, implement, and manage network infrastructure. Ensure network security, performance, and scalability.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Knowledge of networking protocols and hardware.
      ->Experience with network monitoring and troubleshooting tools.
      ->Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹10.0 lakhs to ₹25.0 lakhs per annum.

Job Locations : India Locations: Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Mumbai, Gurugram. Onsite Locations: USA (Redmond, Seattle), Ireland, Canada, Australia, Singapore, UK. Remote Work: Some Microsoft roles offer flexible or hybrid remote options.
