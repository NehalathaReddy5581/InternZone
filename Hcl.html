JOBS OFFERED BY

HCLTech offers a variety of roles for B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (CSE) graduates, providing opportunities to work on diverse projects across multiple domains. Below is an overview of key positions, including role descriptions, required skills, and estimated salary ranges:

  1. Graduate Engineer Trainee (GET)
    • Role Overview:
      As a GET, you will undergo training to develop skills in software development, testing, and maintenance. Post-training, you'll work on real-time projects under the guidance of experienced professionals.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++.
      -Understanding of software development life cycle (SDLC) methodologies.
      -Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.5 to ₹4.75 lakhs per annum, depending on qualifications.
  2. Software Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Develop, test, and deploy software solutions to meet client requirements. Ensure the performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications.
    • Skills Required:
      -Expertise in software development frameworks and tools.
      -Ability to write clean, maintainable, and efficient code.
      -Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹1.7 to ₹8 lakhs per annum
  3. Analyst
    • Role Overview:
      Analyze business processes and requirements to provide data-driven recommendations. Collaborate with stakeholders to implement technology solutions that meet business objectives.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in business analysis and requirement gathering.
      -Experience with project management tools and methodologies.
      -Strong analytical and documentation skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹0.8 to ₹6.5 lakhs per annum
  4. Design Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Engage in the design and development of software systems and applications. Work closely with cross-functional teams to translate user requirements into system designs.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in design tools and software.
      -Strong understanding of design principles and methodologies.
      -Ability to create detailed design documentation.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.5 to ₹4.75 lakhs per annum, depending on qualifications.
  5. IT Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Responsible for managing and maintaining IT infrastructure, ensuring optimal performance and security. Provide technical support and implement new technologies to meet organizational needs.
    • Skills Required:
      -Knowledge of networking protocols and hardware.
      -Experience with system administration and IT support.
      -Problem-solving skills to address IT-related issues promptly.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.5 to ₹4.75 lakhs per annum, depending on qualifications.
  6. Test Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Design and execute test plans to ensure software quality. Identify and document defects, and work with development teams to resolve issues.
    • Skills Required:
      -Experience with performance testing and optimization.
      -Knowledge of test automation tools and frameworks.
      -Strong attention to detail and analytical skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.5 to ₹4.75 lakhs per annum, depending on qualifications.
  7. Infrastructure Management Specialist
    • Role Overview:
      Manage and support the organization's IT infrastructure, including servers, networks, and data centers. Ensure system reliability and implement upgrades as needed.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in infrastructure management tools and software.
      -Experience with cloud platforms and virtualization technologies.
      -Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.5 to ₹4.75 lakhs per annum, depending on qualifications.
  8. Digital Process Operations Associate
    • Role Overview:
      Support digital transformation initiatives by managing and optimizing business processes. Work to improve efficiency and effectiveness through automation and technology integration.
    • Skills Required:
      -Understanding of digital process automation tools.
      -Ability to analyze and optimize business workflows.
      -Strong communication and collaboration skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹1.96 to ₹2.20 lakhs per annum.
