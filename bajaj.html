JOBS OFFERED BY

Bajaj Finance Limited offers a variety of roles for Computer Science Engineering (CSE) graduates, primarily within their Information Technology (IT) and Digital Transformation divisions. Below is an overview of potential positions and their associated salary packages:

  1. Software Engineer / Developer
    • Role Overview:
      Develops and maintains banking applications, ensures software efficiency, and troubleshoots technical issues.
    • Skills Required:
      Java, Python, SQL, cloud computing, API development.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹9,49,630 per annum.
  2. Senior Software Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Leads development projects, mentors junior developers, and ensures the delivery of high-quality software solutions.
    • Skills Required:
      Java, Spring Boot, microservices, cloud platforms, DevOps.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹7.4 Lakhs to ₹22.4 Lakhs per annum.
  3. Backend Software Engineer

    • Skills Required:
      Java, Python, Node.js, SQL, NoSQL, Kubernetes, Docker.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹4,87,000 to ₹13,40,000 per annum.
  4. Microservices Developer

      -> Base Salary: ₹8,15,672 per annum.

      ->Joining Bonus: ₹50,000.

    • Role Overview:

    • Salary Range:
      ₹16,30,000 to ₹19,90,000 per annum.
  6. Machine Learning (ML) Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Develops and implements machine learning models to enhance financial products and services, such as credit scoring and fraud detection.
    • Skills Required:
      Machine learning algorithms, deep learning (TensorFlow, PyTorch), Python, NLP, statistical modeling.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹38,50,000 to ₹40,50,000 per annum.
  7. Associate Lead Data Scientist
    • Role Overview:
      Leads data science projects, collaborates with cross-functional teams, and provides data-driven insights to inform business strategies.
    • Skills Required:
      Data analytics, AI/ML, Python, R, SQL, cloud computing.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹28,60,000 to ₹38,50,000 per annum.
  8. Lead Software Architect
    • Role Overview:
      Designs and oversees the implementation of complex software systems, ensuring alignment with business objectives and scalability requirements.

      ₹44,00,000 per annum.
  9. UI/UX Designer
    • Role Overview:

    • Skills Required:
      Figma, Adobe XD, CSS, JavaScript, React, user research, wireframing.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹7,70,000 to ₹13,50,000 per annum.
  10. Technical Business Analyst
    • Role Overview:
      Acts as a liaison between IT and business units, analyzing business needs and translating them into technical requirements.

      ₹6,00,000 to ₹12,00,000 per annum
