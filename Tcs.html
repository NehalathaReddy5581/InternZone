JOBS OFFERED BY

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) offers a range of roles for B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (CSE) graduates, providing opportunities to work on diverse projects across various industries. Below is an overview of key positions, including role descriptions, required skills, and estimated salary ranges:

  1. Assistant System Engineer Trainee (ASE-T)
    • Role Overview: As an entry-level position, ASE-Ts undergo the Initial Learning Program (ILP) to acquire essential skills in software development, testing, and maintenance. They collaborate with teams to deliver client-specific solutions.
    • Skills Required
      ->Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++.
      ->Understanding of software development life cycle (SDLC) methodologies.
      ->Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹3.36 lakhs per annum.
  2. Assistant System Engineer (ASE)
    • Role Overview: After completing the training period, ASEs engage in designing, developing, and maintaining software applications, ensuring they meet client requirements and quality standards.
    • Skills Required Experience with databases and web services. Ability to write clean, maintainable, and efficient code. Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹3.5 to ₹4.5 lakhs per annum.
  3. Systems Engineer
    • Role Overview: Systems Engineers are responsible for analyzing user requirements, designing system solutions, and coordinating with development teams to implement and test these solutions.
    • Skills Required
      ->Strong understanding of system architecture and design.
      ->Proficiency in system integration and troubleshooting.
      ->Excellent communication skills for client interactions.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹4.2 to ₹5.8 lakhs per annum.
  4. IT Analyst
    • Role Overview: IT Analysts serve as a bridge between clients and technical teams, gathering requirements, analyzing processes, and ensuring that IT solutions align with business objectives.
    • Skills Required
      ->Proficiency in business analysis and requirement gathering.
      ->Experience with project management tools and methodologies.
      ->Strong analytical and documentation skills.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹5.5 to ₹7.5 lakhs per annum.
  5. Software Developer
    • Role Overview: Software Developers at TCS are involved in coding, testing, and deploying applications across various domains, ensuring functionality and performance meet client expectations.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Proficiency in multiple programming languages and frameworks.
      ->Experience with agile development methodologies.
      ->Ability to debug and resolve software issues efficiently.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹4 to ₹6 lakhs per annum.
  6. Data Analyst
    • Role Overview: Data Analysts interpret complex data sets to help organizations make informed business decisions, involving data collection, analysis, and reporting.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Proficiency in data analysis tools like SQL, Excel, and visualization software.
      ->Strong statistical and analytical skills.
      ->Ability to communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹4.5 to ₹6.5 lakhs per annum.
  7. Network Engineer
    • Role Overview: Network Engineers design, implement, and manage network infrastructures, ensuring optimal performance and security.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Knowledge of networking protocols and hardware.
      ->Experience with network security practices.
      ->Problem-solving skills to address network issues promptly.
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹4 to ₹6 lakhs per annum.
  8. Quality Analyst
    • Role Overview: Quality Analysts are responsible for testing software applications to identify defects and ensure that products meet quality standards before deployment.
    • Skills Required:
      ->Experience with manual and automated testing tools.
      ->Strong attention to detail and analytical skills.
      ->Understanding of testing methodologies an
    • Salary Range: Approximately ₹3.8 to ₹5.5 lakhs per annum.
