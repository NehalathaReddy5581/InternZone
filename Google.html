JOBS OFFERED BY

Google offers a diverse range of roles for B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (CSE) graduates, providing opportunities to work on cutting-edge technologies and innovative projects. Below is an overview of ten key positions, including role descriptions, required skills, and estimated Salary ranges:

  1. Software Engineer (L3/L4)
    • Role Overview:
      Design, develop, test, and maintain software applications and systems. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality products.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++.
      -Strong understanding of data structures and algorithms.
      -Experience with software development methodologies.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.56 million to ₹7.87 million per annum, depending on level and experience.
  2. Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)
    • Role Overview:
      Ensure the reliability, performance, and scalability of Google's services. Develop automated solutions for system monitoring and incident response.
    • Skills Required:
      -Experience with system administration and networking.
      -Proficiency in scripting languages like Python or Shell.
      -Knowledge of cloud computing and distributed systems.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.5 million to ₹8 million per annum.
  3. Data Scientist
    • Role Overview:
      Analyze complex datasets to extract actionable insights. Develop models and algorithms to solve business problems and drive decision-making.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in statistical analysis and machine learning.
      -Experience with data analysis tools like R or Python.
      -Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹4 million to ₹9 million per annum.
  4. Product Manager
    • Role Overview:
      Oversee the development and launch of products. Define product vision, gather requirements, and collaborate with engineering, design, and marketing teams.
    • Skills Required:
      -Strong understanding of market trends and user needs.
      -Excellent project management and communication skills.
      -Ability to work cross-functionally and lead teams.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹5 million to ₹12 million per annum.
  5. UX Designer
    • Role Overview:
      Design intuitive and engaging user interfaces. Conduct user research and create wireframes, prototypes, and visual designs.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in design tools like Adobe XD, Figma, or Sketch.
      -Strong understanding of user-centered design principles.
      -Ability to translate user needs into functional designs.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3 million to ₹7 million per annum.
  6. Cloud Solutions Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Design and implement cloud-based solutions for clients. Provide technical guidance and support for cloud migration and deployment.
    • Skills Required:
      -Experience with cloud platforms like Google Cloud Platform (GCP).
      -Proficiency in networking, security, and infrastructure management.
      -Strong problem-solving and client-facing skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹4 million to ₹8 million per annum.
  7. Machine Learning Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Develop and deploy machine learning models and algorithms. Work on projects involving natural language processing, computer vision, and predictive analytics.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
      -Strong programming skills in Python or Java.
      -Experience with data preprocessing and model optimization.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹4.5 million to ₹10 million per annum.
  8. Network Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Design, implement, and manage network infrastructure. Ensure network security, performance, and scalability.
    • Skills Required:
      -Knowledge of networking protocols and hardware.
      -Experience with network monitoring and troubleshooting tools.
      -Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3 million to ₹6 million per annum.
  9. Technical Program Manager
    • Role Overview:
      Coordinate and manage technical projects from inception to completion. Work with engineering teams to deliver projects on time and within scope.
    • Skills Required:
      -Strong project management and organizational skills.
      -Technical background in software development or engineering.
      -Excellent communication and leadership abilities.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹5 million to ₹11 million per annum.
  10. Security Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      Identify and mitigate security vulnerabilities in systems and applications. Develop security protocols and conduct regular security assessments.
    • Skills Required:
      -Knowledge of cybersecurity principles and practices.
      -Experience with security tools and technologies.
      -Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹4 million to ₹9 million per annum.
