JOBS OFFERED BY

Deloitte offers a range of opportunities for B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (CSE) graduates, enabling them to work on diverse projects across various industries. Below is an overview of key positions, including role descriptions, required skills, and estimated salary ranges:

  1. Analyst
    • Role Overview:
      As an Analyst, you will engage in research, data analysis, and the development of reports to support client projects. This role involves collaborating with senior team members to deliver insights and solutions tailored to client needs.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in data analysis tools and methodologies.
      -Strong communication and presentation skills.
      -Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹6.5 lakhs per annum.
  2. Software Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      In this role, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining software applications that meet client requirements. This includes coding, testing, and deploying applications, as well as troubleshooting and optimizing performance.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++.
      -Experience with software development life cycle (SDLC) methodologies.
      -Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹3.9 to ₹16.2 lakhs per annum, depending on experience and performance.
  3. Business Technology Analyst
    • Role Overview:
      As a Business Technology Analyst, you will analyze business processes and identify technology-based solutions to improve efficiency and effectiveness. This role involves working closely with clients to understand their needs and translating them into technical requirements.
    • Skills Required:
      -Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
      -Understanding of business processes and technology integration.
      -Effective communication skills to liaise between technical and non-technical stakeholders.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹6.5 lakhs per annum.
  4. Data Analyst
    • Role Overview:
      Data Analysts at Deloitte are responsible for collecting, processing, and performing statistical analyses on large datasets. The goal is to discover patterns and insights that can inform business decisions.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in data analysis tools such as SQL, Excel, and data visualization software.
      -Strong statistical and analytical skills.
      -Ability to communicate complex data findings in a clear and concise manner.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹6.5 lakhs per annum.
  5. Cyber Security Analyst
    • Role Overview:
      In this position, you will be responsible for identifying and mitigating security threats to client systems and networks. This includes conducting security assessments, monitoring for security breaches, and developing strategies to enhance security measures.
    • Skills Required:
      -Knowledge of cybersecurity principles and practices.
      -Experience with security assessment tools and methodologies.
      -Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
    • Salary Range:
      Approximately ₹6.5 lakhs per annum.
  6. Cloud Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      -Responsible for designing, deploying, and managing cloud-based applications and infrastructure.
      -Work with clients to implement cloud solutions that align with their business goals.
      -Optimize cloud performance, security, and cost efficiency.
    • Skills Required:
      -Proficiency in AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
      -Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
      -Knowledge of cloud security best practices.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹8-14 LPA
  7. AI & Machine Learning Engineer
    • Role Overview:
      -Develop AI and ML models to automate business processes and enhance decision-making.
      -Work on natural language processing (NLP), computer vision, or predictive analytics.
      -Collaborate with data scientists and engineers to deploy AI models into production.
    • Skills Required:
      -Strong knowledge of Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn.
      -Experience with data preprocessing and model training.
      -Ability to fine-tune models for performance optimization.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹9-18 LPA
  8. ERP Consultant (SAP/Oracle)
    • Role Overview:
      -Implement and customize SAP or Oracle ERP solutions for clients.
      -Analyze business requirements and translate them into technical configurations.
      -Provide support, troubleshooting, and maintenance for ERP systems.
    • Skills Required: -Understanding of SAP/Oracle ERP modules (Finance, HR, Supply Chain, etc.).
      -Knowledge of SQL and database management.
      -Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work with business users.
    • Salary Range:
      ₹7-15 LPA
